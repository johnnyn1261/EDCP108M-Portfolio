EDCP108M (Concepts and Skills: Math Study Skills and Building Confidence) is a half-semester course offered at the University of Maryland, College Park.

Like all EDCP courses, this course is geared towards helping students with College and Career Advancement. For EDCP108M in particular, this course is designed for students who are experiencing difficulty in learning math, or for students studying math education. For more information about EDCP108M and other EDCP courses offered throughout the year, click the button to learn more.
